    How To Prevent Fire
    Watch cooking Anything could happen when you leave your kitchen unattended while cooking. It's very easy for fire to spread if you don't stop it early on. That's why you should never leave your kitchen while cooking.
    Maintain wires Make sure that the wires you use aren't torn or broken before plugging them in. If they're plugged in while broken, it could cause a fire.
    Fire Extuinguisher Incase a fire ever happends in your house, having a fire extuinguisher will certainly help keep you safe.
    Store flammable objects properly Accidentally leaving a flammable object near a flame or a heart source can cause fires that's why you should store them in proper places.
